Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/2 cup almonds
  • 1 teaspoon of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup oatmeal
  • 1/3 cup walnuts
  • pear
  • peach
  • black raspberries

Instructions:

Crush almonds and knead the topping with the rest of topping ingredients. Cut fruits into smaller pieces. Put them into small ovenproof dishes. Cover the fruit with the topping. Bake about 30 minutes until the topping gets gold and the fruit boil in their juices. Serve hot with some good coffee or with ice cream. Share with those you love!
